Presbyterian Women

Church Life > Organisations > Presbyterian Women

Presbyterian Women encourages women to become disciples of Christ and aims to highlight the need for: love and unity, obedience to God, Christian living and spiritual maturity, service using gifts, time and money, local and global mission.

The monthly group meetings include:

  • A short devotional time
  • Updates about missionaries
  • A spiritual or secular topic with a guest or home-grown speaker(s)
  • Lots of fun
  • Time to chat over a cuppa with opportunity to get to know each other

In addition to the monthly meetings, there also are:

  • Some fundraising for the PW Mission Fund
  • An annual PW Service
  • Get-togethers with other PW groups
  • Participation in Women's World Day of Prayer
  • A spring evening outing
  • A summer day trip
