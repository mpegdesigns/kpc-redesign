Youth Club

Church Life > Organisations > Youth Club

The Youth Club is an informal club for young people. There are many activities on offer, including:

  • Indoor & outdoor sports like football, hockey, dodgeball, basketball, badminton etc.
  • Table tennis
  • Snooker & pool
  • Table football
  • Air hockey
  • Video games

In addition to this, there are also theme nights, crafts, talks, and sleepovers.

Meeting Time
Friday 7:30pm - 9:30pm

Designed For
Young people of P7 and all Secondary school ages
