Bowling Club

Church Life > Organisations > Bowling Club

The bowling club is an organisation which provides fellowship, friendship, and the opportunity to play bowls (or learn how).

The Monday evening group is a member of the Sixmilewater & District Bowling League East Antrim Zone. They have League and Club Cup Matches, Charity Cup Matches, and an Annual Tournament.

The Thursday afternoon group plays Club Competitions and Cups. There are also a small number of friendly matches with similar clubs.

Fees are as follows:

  • Monday Evening: £15.00 annual subscription
  • Thursday Afternoon: £6.00 yearly membership fee, plus 50p per afternoon (non-members: £1.00)
