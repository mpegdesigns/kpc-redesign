Bible Class

Church Life > Organisations > Bible Class

The Bible Classes are a Sunday Morning organisation for people of Secondary School age. Their aim is to build upon the foundations taught in Sunday School and help the young people to further understand the Bible and Christianity.

The Bible classes are split into two sections:

  • Junior: This is for young people in 1st and 2nd year. They meet in the Youth Wing in the Church Halls.
  • Senior: This is for young people in 3rd year and upwards. They meet in the Kilbride Room in the Church Halls.

Throughout the year, there are several special church services in which the Bible classes participate:

  • Christmas Service: Help the children of the Sunday School to celebrate the Christmas story.
  • Children's Day Service: This is an opportunity for the Bible Class to share what they have been learning about during the year in a special service held in June.
