Ramblers

Church Life > Organisations > Ramblers

Kilbride Ramblers are affiliated to the 'Walking in Your Community' walk scheme. We are a friendly group of like-minded people who share a passio for hte outdoors. There are walks of varying length.

The Wednesday walks run from May - September. They are mostly local, about 3 - 4 miles in length, and usually end with a cup of tea.

The Saturday walks typically run on the 3rd Saturday of every month, and ususally finish with a coffee or evening meal. These are advertised in the Bulletin.

Meeting Time
Wednesday Walks: Wednesday 7:15pm, Church car park
Saturday Walks: see Bulletin

Designed For
Anyone interested in walking and enjoy meeting others
