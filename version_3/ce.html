Christian Endeavour

Church Life > Organisations > Christian Endeavour

Christian Endeavour (C.E.) is an organisation designed to encourage children to put their faith in Jesus Christ, and then help them to grow in that faith.

In the Junior C.E. this is done using a variety of activities; Bible Stories, quizzes, sword drill, singing, and drama. This allows the children to engage in a fun and interactive way.

In the Senior C.E. the young people are encouraged to participate in Bible Study and discussions. They also have a variety of special spearkers throughout the year, showing them how they can apply what they have been learning in their everyday lives. There are also occasionally outings and integration with other C.E. groups.
