Parents and Toddlers Group

Church Life > Organisations > Parents and Toddlers

Kilbride Parents and Toddlers Group is an informat setting where adults can get together to chat and make new friends. It is also the ideal situation for hte pre-school children to socialise and learn important values such as sharing and taking turns, under the supervision of their accompanying adult.

There are around 25-30 adults and 30 children each week and new members are always welcome, whether the attend Kilbride Church or not.
