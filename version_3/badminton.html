Badminton

Church Life > Organisations > Badminton

The badminton club provides an opportunity for fun team play, for those just starting out and those who have been playing for a long time. There are two sessions; the junior club, held on a Friday and the senior club, held on a Tuesday.

Membership fees are as follows:

  • Juniors: 50p per session.
  • Seniors: £1.00 per night, or £10 per session.
