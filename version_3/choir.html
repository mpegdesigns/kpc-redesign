Choir

Worship > Choir

If you want to share God's love with others through singing, you are warmly invited to participate in the ministry of music. This can be done by joining the choir and the worship group.

The chior performs during special services throughout the year (and there are approximately 12 choir practices a year).

The worship groups work on a rota, with four members singing at the front of church each Sunday. A practice takes place 30 minutes before the start of the service.

The ability to read music is not necessary for participation in either the choir or worship group.
