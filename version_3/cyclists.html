Kilbride Cyclists

Church Life > Organisations > Kilbride Cyclists

"Kilbride Cyclists" was formed in early 2007 when a group of men from Kilbride Presbyterian Church, just outside Ballyclare, decided they needed to get fitter and one way to do this was to start cycling. There are now nearly 40 members to the club. They regularly hold events for charity.

Below is a feed from the Facebook group, or more information can be found at http://www.kilbridecyclists.co.uk

Meeting Time
