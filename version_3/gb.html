Girls Brigade

Church Life > Organisations > Girls Brigade

The Girls Brigade at Kilbride incorporates a large number of activities for girls of all ages. The girls are divided by age as follows (please note, all ages given refer to age at 1st July):

  • Tiny Tots: Girls aged 3 - 5 years
  • Explorers: Girls aged 5 - 8 years
  • Juniors: Girls aged 8 - 11 years
  • Seniors: Girls aged 11 - 13 years
  • Brigaders: Girls aged 14 - 18 years

The girls take part in various activities depending upon their age group. These include:

  • Badgework
  • Bible Class
  • Marching
  • P.E.
  • Drill
  • Craft
  • Competitions
  • Cooking (Seniors and Brigaders only)
  • Duke of Edinburgh (Seniors and Brigaders only)

Please Note: The start date is a uniform night only, but DOES count towards attendance badge.
