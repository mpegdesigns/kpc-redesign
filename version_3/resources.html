Resources

Discipleship > Resources

There are several external resources which members of the congregation may find useful.

Presbyterian Herald

This is a magazine published by Presbyterian Church in Ireland. It features articles about the Presbyterian Church in Ireland; events, ministers, sister Churches, letters, and mission work. There are 10 issues produced per year.

Wider World

The Wider World is an excellent magazine very closely associated with the P.W. and P.C.I. It has very good articles about the church and its work at home and overseas. There are articles written by P.C.I. Deaconesses and Missionaries and a good children's page. There are 4 issues per year.

Points for Prayer

The Points for PRayer bookelt has been totally revamped and will start from September 2014 through to August 2015. It will follow a 52 week cycle and will be more user friendly. Copies will be available in September.

Word for Today

The Word for Today are quarterly Bible Reading Notes produced by UCB. These are available at the start of each quarter.
