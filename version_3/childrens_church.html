Children's Church & Créche

Church Life > Organisations > Children's Church & Créche

The créche provides an environment of supervised play for children so parents can attend the church service. Parents can choose to either leave their children into créche before Church, or at any time during the service.

It would be helpful if parents were able to provide items they feel their child would need, such as; nappies, dummies, bottles, snacks etc. Please also tell helpers if your child has any allergies or medical conditions they need to be made aware of.

Children up to P4 go to Children's Church each Sunday after the children's hymn for their own activities. They meet in the vestiblue of the Church and are escorted to the Church Hall. Parents must sign their children in on the sheet in the vestibule.

Children aged P5 to P7 also leave on the first Sunday of every month for their own activities in the hall. On other Sundays, there is a worksheet available in the vestibule.

If the parents of children either in the créche or Children's Church feel they could volunteer to be part of the rota, please get in touch with one of the leaders.
