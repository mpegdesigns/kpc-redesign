Welcome to Kilbride Presbyterian Church

Kilbride Presbyterian Church was formed in 1848 and has since been serving the community of Kilbride and the surrounding areas. This website provides information about life within the congregation, as well as providing resources for those wishing to be involved in the day to day life of the church.
